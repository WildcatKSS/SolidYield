# B2C Acquisition Channels

Consumer counterpart to the outbound B2B templates (`cold-email-templates.md`, `linkedin-outreach.md`). Fill in the bracketed fields after product selection; validate one channel at a time and measure cost per signup before scaling.

## Channel Shortlist

| Channel | Best For | First Test | Cost Signal to Watch |
|---|---|---|---|
| Communities (Reddit, Discord, Facebook groups, forums) | Niche pains with active discussion | Answer real threads about [problem], link a landing page in profile/bio where rules allow | Signups per hour invested |
| Short-form content (TikTok, Reels, Shorts, X) | Visually demonstrable problems | 5–10 posts showing [pain] and the fix | Views → landing-page clicks → signups |
| SEO / long-form content | Problems people search for | 3 articles targeting [problem] keywords | Impressions → clicks → signups |
| App store listing | Mobile-first products | Optimized listing + screenshots | Store views → installs |
| Paid ads (small budget) | Pricing and message testing | €50–100 test on one platform, one audience | Cost per signup vs. target CAC |
| Waitlist + referral | Pre-launch demand proof | Landing page with waitlist and share incentive | Visitor-to-signup conversion (target 5%+) |
| Product directories (Product Hunt and similar) | Launch spikes and early adopters | Launch post with clear one-line promise | Signups retained after 7 days |

## Community Post Template

Context: post in threads where people describe [problem]; follow the community's self-promotion rules.

> I kept running into [problem] and couldn't find a good fix, so I'm building [one-line product promise]. Not selling anything here — genuinely curious how others handle this today? (Happy to share what I'm building if anyone wants to try it.)

## Waitlist Landing Page Checklist

- One-sentence promise stating [outcome] for [target user]
- The price (or price range) visible before signup — hides false demand
- Single call to action: join the waitlist / pre-order
- Measured per channel (UTM or equivalent) so conversion is attributable

## Rules

- Every channel test gets a target (signups, conversion, cost) before it starts.
- Record results in `docs/business/validation-plan.md` evidence; do not invent numbers.
- Channels that cannot be repeated at acceptable cost do not count as an acquisition path.
