# Validation Plan

## Objective

Validate willingness to pay before building.

## Minimum Evidence Required

Use the column matching the customer type chosen at product selection. Consumer price points are low, so B2C needs larger-sample signals than B2B interviews alone.

### B2B

- 10 customer interviews
- 5 confirmed painful-problem responses
- 2 strong payment-intent signals
- 1 pilot/pre-order/paid-beta signal or clear buying process

### B2C

- 15 user interviews or structured survey responses confirming the pain
- Landing page test with measured visitor-to-signup conversion (target: 5%+ from a defined channel)
- 25+ waitlist signups from a channel that is repeatable at acceptable cost
- 5+ pre-orders or paid-beta commitments at the target price

## Methods

- Customer interviews
- Cold outreach
- Landing page
- Pricing test
- Competitor pricing research

## Pass/Fail Criteria

Pass only if evidence suggests a realistic path to first paying customers.
