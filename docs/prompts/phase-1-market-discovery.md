# Phase 1 Prompt – Market Discovery

You are still product-neutral.

Analyze 10 SaaS or digital-service opportunities (B2B, B2C, or a mix) that meet:
- Real customer pain (business or consumer)
- Paying customer
- Buildable by one developer
- MVP possible in 60 days
- Path to €1.000 MRR in 12 months
- Not ad-dependent
- Not marketplace-dependent
- Not crypto/gambling/dropshipping

For each:
- Problem
- Target customer
- Current alternatives
- Why customers pay
- Distribution channel
- Competitive pressure
- Build complexity
- MRR potential
- Risks

Score 1-10:
- Payment willingness
- Urgency
- Time to first customer
- Build simplicity
- MRR potential
- Organic distribution

Only shortlist ideas scoring 50/60+.

Do not invent validation evidence. Mark assumptions clearly.
